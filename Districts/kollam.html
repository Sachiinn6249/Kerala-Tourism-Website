Kollam

Kollam has been the centre of cashew industry in Kerala from the time
of the Portuguese in 16th c. Having hundreds of working cashew
factories in the district, Kollam still continues to be the largest processed
cashew exporter in India..

About

Kollam is a city in the state of Kerala, on India's Malabar Coast. It’s known as a trade hub and for its beaches, like lively Kollam and secluded Thirumullavaram. Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Police Museum has artifacts tracing the history of the police force.

Nearby, Ashtamudi Lake is a gateway to the Kerala backwaters, a network of waterways rich with vegetation. The striped 1902 Tangasseri Lighthouse has ocean views.
